Some Dangers of Drug Abuse:

  • Physical Harm: Damages organs like heart, liver, lungs; risks heart attacks, respiratory failure.
  • Mental Health Issues: Causes or worsens anxiety, depression, psychosis.
  • Addiction: Leads to dependency, disrupting daily life.
  • Social Breakdown: Strains relationships, causes isolation, job loss.
  • Legal/Safety Risks: Leads to arrests, accidents, or overdose.

Healthy Alternatives to Drug Use:

Exercise
Meditation
Creative Hobbies
Social Support
Healthy Diet
